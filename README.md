## 📱 About the Game

Three Card Poker delivers an authentic poker experience with simple rules and fast-paced gameplay. Each round begins with an initial bet, after which both the player and the dealer receive three cards. Players must evaluate their hand and decide whether to continue by placing a play bet or fold and forfeit the round.

Winning is determined by traditional three-card poker hand rankings, rewarding players who make smart decisions and build stronger hands than the dealer.

Hand rankings include:

- Straight Flush
- Three of a Kind
- Straight
- Flush
- Pair
- High Card

## 🚀 How It Works

1. Place your initial bet.
2. Receive three cards.
3. Review your hand.
4. Choose to **Play** or **Fold**.
5. The dealer reveals their cards.
6. The higher-ranking hand wins the round.
